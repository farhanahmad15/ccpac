About CCPAC

The Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is a student-driven organization dedicated to fostering curiosity and exploration in the fields of physics and astronomy.

We provide a platform for students to engage in meaningful scientific discussions, collaborative learning, and hands-on activities that go beyond the traditional classroom experience.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of scientists, thinkers, and innovators through regular academic sessions, problem-solving challenges, and community-building events.

Membership

Who Can Join?

Membership at the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club is open to all students of Chittagong College who have an interest in physics and astronomy. We believe that curiosity is more important than prior expertise.

Whether you are just beginning your journey in science or already passionate about understanding the universe, CCPAC provides a welcoming space for learning and growth.

Why You Should Join CCPAC?

  • Gain exposure to physics and astronomy through regular academic sessions led by seniors and alumni
  • Improve your problem-solving and critical thinking skills through the Weekly Physics Challenge
  • Develop scientific writing and communication skills through article writing activities
  • Connect with like-minded students who share an interest in science and exploration
  • Receive guidance and inspiration for higher studies and research in science-related fields
  • Experience learning beyond textbooks and classrooms in an engaging and collaborative environment

CCPAC Journal

CTG College Physics & Astronomy Journal

Embark on a voyage through the frontiers of scientific erudition with the CTG College Physics & Astronomy Journal!

Brought to you by the Chittagong College Physics & Astronomy Club, this journal is a panoply of cerebral explorations in physics and astronomy. Delve into esoteric theoretical breakthroughs, empirical investigations, perspicacious scientific writings, and imaginative sci-fi narratives meticulously curated by our most prodigious students.

Aspiring contributors from other institutions are cordially invited to augment our compendium with their insights and discoveries, enriching this encyclopedic repository of nascent scientific thought.
